About Shanghai

Shanghai is China’s largest city, a global financial hub, and a symbol of rapid development.

From a small fishing town to an international metropolis, it blends Eastern traditions with Western influences.

Today, it stands as a vibrant cultural melting pot, offering world-class attractions and cuisine.

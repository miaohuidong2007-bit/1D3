Famous Attractions

The Bund

Shanghai's most iconic landmark, with historic colonial buildings along the Huangpu River.

Jing'an Temple

A stunning golden Buddhist temple in the heart of modern Shanghai.

Wukang Building

An iconic Art Deco landmark in the French Concession.

Shanghai Disneyland

The first Disney resort in mainland China, home to the magical Enchanted Storybook Castle.

Disney Night Show

Stunning fireworks and light show over the castle every night.

Historic Colonial Architecture

Beautiful European-style buildings in the Bund area, showcasing Shanghai's colonial history.
